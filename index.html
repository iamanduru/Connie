About Connie

A space where healing begins and real change unfolds.

I’m a psychologist with a passion for helping people untangle their thoughts, manage stress, and live fuller lives. Let’s be honest,life can get messy. One day you’re on top of things, the next you’re spiraling at 2 AM wondering if your thoughts will ever slow down. That’s where I come in.

With over 1½ years of clinical experience, and a strong focus on creating safe, inclusive spaces, especially for LGBTQIA+ individuals, I offer compassionate, affirming therapy that meets you exactly where you are.

Whether you’re feeling stuck, navigating anxiety, or working through relationship challenges, I provide evidence-based support using tools like CBT, mindfulness, and trauma-informed care to guide your journey. You can show up exactly as you are , no judgment, no pressure. Just real conversations, real support, and the space to grow at your own pace.
